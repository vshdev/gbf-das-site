About Us

Your Eyes Deserve the Best

Dr. Ashok Sharma is a renowned ophthalmologist dedicated to restoring and preserving vision for patients across Jammu & Kashmir.

We believe that high-quality eye care should be accessible and comfortable. Our clinic combines medical expertise with a warm, patient-friendly environment. From routine computerized checkups to complex cataract surgeries, we ensure safety and precision at every step.

Our Expertise

Specialized Treatments

We provide a full spectrum of ophthalmology services using the latest diagnostic and surgical equipment.

Computerized Checkup

Precise vision testing using auto-refractometers followed by manual verification for 100% accuracy.

Cataract Surgery

Suture-less Phacoemulsification cataract surgery with premium IOL implants for quick recovery.

Glaucoma Mgmt

Early detection, pressure monitoring, and medical/surgical management to prevent vision loss.

OCT & Biometry

High-resolution retinal imaging (OCT) and optical biometry for accurate surgical planning.
